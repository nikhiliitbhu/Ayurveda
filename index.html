➤ What are these body types?

our ancient sciences share that everything in this universe is made up of the five great elements. Earth, Water, Fire, Air and Space. Weather it's a plant, a chair, your t-shirt, or even the phone that you're currently holding is all made of these five ingredients. But here's the fascinating part. These five elements are not just outside of you, they are also inside you. In your body all the solid structures, bonus, flush, skin are made of the earth element. All fluids, your saliva, urine, blood, sweat are the water element. The heat that keeps you warm, that transforms your food into smaller molecules, that's the fire element. The life-giving breath that you're inhaling and gulping, even right now, that's the air element. And the various empty spaces in your body, in your arteries, in your intestines, in your nasal passages, that's the space element. You have all five elements inside you. That's what your physical body is made of. But these five elements are all in equal proportion, well, no. In each person their balance is different. This difference creates the 3 doshas.


➤ The 3 doshas: Vata, Pitta, Kapha.

Vata Body Type

When the air and space elements dominate in someone's body, they're called a vata body type. Vata people are just like the wind, fast moving both in their body and thoughts.

Pitta Body Type

When fire and water elements dominate, they are called the Pitta body type. Pitta people are just like the fire, piercing, hot, intense, passionate and can even get angry easily.

Kapha Body Type

When water and earth dominate, they are called a Kapha body type. Kapha people adjust like the earth, stable, heavy, grounded and calm. Both physically and emotionally.

This unique mix of elements within you is called your body type, or your nature.
